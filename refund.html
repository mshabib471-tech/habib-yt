Refund Policy

At Habib YT, refunds are available only under specific conditions.

Refund Eligibility

Refund is provided only if your order is not delivered due to:

  • Stock issues
  • API/Server failure
  • Technical problems

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Wrong game ID provided by the customer
  • Wrong profile link
  • Completed/Delivered orders
  • Fake or fraudulent bKash trxID

How Refund Works

  1. Customer must provide the valid bKash TrxID
  2. We verify the payment
  3. Refund is sent back through bKash within 24–72 hours
